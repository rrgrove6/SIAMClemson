Events

If you have further questions on any of the events listed, please contact one of the officers.

  • September 20, 2007: Mathematical Sciences Departmental Fall Picnic

    5:00 pm, Clemson outdoor lab. Please look out for the registration form in your mailbox or download it here!

    To kick off the semester, the SIAM Student Chapter will be sponsoring our annual fall picnic. This event is open to all faculty, staff, and graduate students as well as their families. We especially encourage all the new students and faculty to come so they may meet returning students, faculty, and staff. This semester the festivities will feature an authentic Brunswick Stew made by Stewmaster Woody and Chief Potato Peeler Fisher, as well as pork and chicken barbeque, side-dishes, pizza, several desserts made by Peter Kiessler and drinks. You are encouraged to bring homemade side dishes and desserts. Hope to see everyone on Thursday the 20th!

  • July 16-20, 2007: ICIAM in Zürich, Switzerland

    6th International Congress on Industrial and Applied Mathematics, Zürich, Switzerland.
    Please note: there is the possibility of receiving financial travel support from SIAM.

  • May 4-5, 2007: Annual SIAM Southeast Atlantic Section Meeting

    31st Annual SIAM-SEAS Meeting, The University of Memphis, Memphis, Tennessee (list of former conferences).

  • April 13, 2007: Mathematical Sciences Departmental Spring Picnic

    5:00 pm, Clemson outdoor lab. Please look out for the registration form in your mailbox or download it here!

    Spring is here and with that comes the annual spring picnic sponsored by Clemson’s SIAM Chapter. The picnic is open for all faculty, staff, graduate students and their families, so we hope you’ll join us. This semester the festivities will return to the picnic area at the Clemson Outdoor Labs Amphitheater and Picnic Area. Bring your Frisbee, soccer ball, football, volleyball etc. if you would like to take part in some recreational activities. SIAM will provide pizza, fried chicken and barbecue as well as the drinks. Hopefully we will have many people choose to bring other dishes and desserts so that there will be a delicious variety of foods to choose from! So, come on join us for some Friday fun!

  • December 2-3, 2006: First ACES Workshop

    First SIAM Southeast Regional Student Conference, Emory University, Atlanta, Georgia (see the ACES announcement and review in SIAM News).

    This workshop is organized by the SIAM student chapter presidents at Emory and Clemson University and will bring together students from the universities at Auburn, Clemson, Emory, and Southcarolina (ACES). If you are interested in attending or giving a presentation, please contact Alexander Engau.

  • September 29 - October 1, 2006: Cha-Cha Days

    Southeastern Atlantic Mathematical Sciences Workshop, College of Charleston, South Carolina.

  • September 8, 2006: Math Sciences Department’s Fall Kick Off

    5:00 pm, Clemson outdoor lab, tell us you are coming!

    Welcome back! To kick off the semester, we will be having our annual fall picnic. This event is open to all faculty, staff, and graduate students as well as their families. We especially encourage all the new students and faculty to come so they may meet returning students, faculty, and staff. This semester the festivities will return to the picnic area at the Clemson Outdoor Labs Amphitheater and Picnic Area. Bring your Frisbee, soccer ball, football, volleyball etc., if you would like to take part in some recreational activities. SIAM will provide barbecue, chicken, pizza and drinks. Hopefully we will have many people bring side dishes and desserts so that there is a lot to choose from. Hope to see everyone on Friday the 8th!

  • April 21, 2006: Math Departmental Spring Picnic

    Theme "Foods of the world", 5:00 pm, Clemson outdoor lab.

  • April 18, 2006: Special Guest Lecture at University of South Carolina

    "The pseudo-transient continuation method for solving nonlinear equations," Tim Kelley, N.C. State University, 3:30 p.m., LeConte College, Room 412.

  • March 30 - April 1, 2006: Annual SIAM South East Atlantic Section Meeting

    Joint 85th MAA Southeastern Section and 30th SIAM Southeastern Atlantic Section Annual Meeting, Auburn University, Auburn, Alabama.
