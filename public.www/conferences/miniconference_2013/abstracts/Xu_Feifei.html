Peridynamics: A Nonlocal Form for Physical Laws
Feifei Xu

The mathematical laws of physics are generally written in terms of derivatives. Computations based on such laws become difficult or impractical when the material under study includes point loads, material interfaces, or cracks. Peridynamics uses an integral formulation to model physical laws, and can naturally handle cases in which the differential approach fails. My research group has implemented the peridynamics approach for 1D and 2D problems. I will report on previous results of our group, and on the further research that I plan to do in this area.
