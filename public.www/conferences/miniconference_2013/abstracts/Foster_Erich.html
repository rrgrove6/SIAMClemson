A conforming finite element solution to the pure streamfunction form of the quasi-geostrophic equations of the ocean
Erich Foster

The quasi-geostrophic equations (QGE) are a commonly used model for the large scale wind-driven ocean circulation. The streamfunction form of QGE involves a fourth order PDEs, thus for a conforming finite element (FE) formulation one requires a C^1 FE. We present a conforming FE discretization of the streamfunction form of the streamfunction form of the QGE, including optimal error estimates and numerical experiments.
