A Two-Level Finite Element Discretization for the Quasi-Geostrophic Equations
David Wells

The Quasigeostrophic Equations are commonly used in the numerical simulation of large scale wind-driven ocean flows. As the geometry of the ocean is complicated, the finite element method is appropriate for accurate depictions of boundaries.We will discuss basic implementation for Argyris finite elements and the use of two-level techniques to linearize a system. This coupled approach (high order finite elements and two levellinearization) will decrease computational time without compromising accuracy.
