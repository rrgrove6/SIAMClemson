Rules
  • We will be playing in teams by table.
  • There are 5 rounds in the game.
  • Each round has 3 categories: Word, People, and Acronym.
  • A word associated with each category will be given and you will have about 5 minutes as a team to come up with a definition (real or phony) for each word.
  • If your team provides the correct definition for a word, you will receive 3 points for that category.
